Question 2.1
Why are the variables declared as private?

Answer:
The variables are declared as private to protect the data from direct access and to achieve encapsulation.

--------------------------------------------------

Question 2.2
What happens if the variables are declared public?

Answer:
Any class can directly access and modify the variables, which may lead to invalid or inconsistent data.

--------------------------------------------------

Question 2.3
Why do we use getters and setters?

Answer:
Getters are used to read private variables, while setters are used to modify them safely and in a controlled way.
